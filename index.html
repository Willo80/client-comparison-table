Instructions

Welcome to the BloomCraft Gardens client comparison table. Here’s how you can use this table to complete the activities:

  1. Review Client Profiles: Look at the detailed information provided for each client, including current needs, behaviours, project history, financials, and future goals.
  2. Assess Criteria: For each client, use the drop-down menus to assess their Budget, Project History, and Potential for Growth based on the provided information.
  3. Calculate Total Score: The total score for each client will be automatically calculated based on the weighted values of the criteria you selected.
  4. 9-Box Grid Placement: Use the total score to place each client in the appropriate position on the 9-Box Grid.
  5. Customer Segmentation: Determine the appropriate segment for each client based on their behaviours and needs.
  6. Adoption Stage: Identify where each client stands in their journey with BloomCraft’s services.

Use the tabs above to get additional help and definitions for each criterion.

Matt's Help

Hi there! Need some help? Here are a few tips to guide you through the activities:

  • Budget: Check the "Detailed Financials" section of each client's profile to understand their budget. A higher budget indicates a higher potential score.
  • Project History: Look at the "Project History" section to see the size and impact of past projects. Major and significant strategic projects will have higher scores.
  • Potential for Growth: Refer to the "Future Goals" section to gauge the client's potential for future growth. Clients with ambitious future goals have higher growth potential scores.
  • Total Score: This is automatically calculated for you based on the selected criteria. Use this score to determine the client’s placement on the 9-Box Grid. Use this for the stage 1 activity
  • Customer Segmentation: Segment clients based on their current needs and behaviors. The "Current Needs" and "Client Behaviours" sections will help you here. Use this for the stage 2 activity
  • Adoption Stage: Identify the client’s adoption stage by looking at their engagement with BloomCraft’s services. This can be found in the "Client Behaviours" section. Use this for the stage 3 activity

Remember, each client's full profile provides all the information you need to make your decisions. Good luck!

Definitions for Each Criterion

Budget: The client's total budget allocated for garden design projects.
Indicators: Explicitly stated in the "Detailed Financials" section of the client profile.
Scoring: $0-$10,000 (1), $10,001-$20,000 (2), $20,001-$30,000 (3), $30,001+ (4)

Project History: The scope and significance of past projects completed with BloomCraft.
Indicators: "Project History" section details the size and impact of projects, determining if they are minor, moderate, major, or significant strategic projects.
Scoring: Minor projects (1), Moderate projects (2), Major projects (3), Significant strategic projects (4)

Potential for Growth: The client’s readiness and potential for future expansion and project involvement.
Indicators: "Future Goals" section indicates whether the client's growth potential is low, medium, high, or very high.
Scoring: Low (1), Medium (2), High (3), Very High (4)

Total Score: A composite score calculated from the weighted values of the above criteria, determining the client's placement on the 9-Box Grid.
Calculation: The total score is automatically calculated by combining the weighted scores of budget, project history, and potential for growth.
