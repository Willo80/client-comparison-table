Matt's Help

Need assistance? Use these tips to guide you:

  • Budget: Refer to the Detailed Financials section to assess the client's total budget.
  • Engagement Level: Look at Client Behaviours and Engagement Level to determine how actively the client engages with BloomCraft.
  • Project History: Review the Project History section to understand the size and impact of past projects.
  • Potential for Growth: Check Future Goals and Strategic Alignment to evaluate potential growth.
  • Strategic Alignment: Assess how well the client's goals align with BloomCraft’s strategic initiatives, based on Current Needs and Future Goals.

Definitions for Each Criterion

  • Budget: The client's total budget allocated for garden design projects.
    • Indicators: Explicitly stated in the "Detailed Financials" section of the client profile.
    • Scoring: $0-$10,000 (1), $10,001-$20,000 (2), $20,001-$30,000 (3), $30,001+ (4)
  • Engagement Level: The level of interaction and collaboration between the client and BloomCraft.
    • Indicators: "Client Behaviours" and "Engagement Level" sections in the profile indicate if the engagement is low, medium, or high.
    • Scoring: Low (1), Medium (2), High (3), Very High (4)
  • Project History: The scope and significance of past projects completed with BloomCraft.
    • Indicators: "Project History" section details the size and impact of projects, determining if they are minor, moderate, major, or significant strategic projects.
    • Scoring: Minor projects (1), Moderate projects (2), Major projects (3), Significant strategic projects (4)
  • Potential for Growth: The client’s readiness and potential for future expansion and project involvement.
    • Indicators: "Future Goals" section indicates whether the client's growth potential is low, medium, or high.
    • Scoring: Low (1), Medium (2), High (3), Very High (4)
  • Strategic Alignment: The extent to which the client's goals and values align with BloomCraft’s strategic objectives.
    • Indicators: Found in the "Competitive Landscape" and "Future Goals" sections, stating how closely the client's goals match BloomCraft’s sustainability initiatives, innovation alignment, etc.
    • Scoring: Low (1), Medium (2), High (3), Very High (4)
  • Total Score: A composite score calculated from the weighted values of the above criteria, determining the client's placement on the 9-Box Grid.
    • Calculation: The total score is automatically calculated by combining the weighted scores of budget, engagement level, project history, potential for growth, and strategic alignment.
